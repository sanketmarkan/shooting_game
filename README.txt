Controls:
	A - cannon move above
	B - cannon move below
	W - cannon move up
	E - cannon move down
	UP - zoom in 
	DOWN - zoom out
	LEFT - span left
	RIGHT - span right
	F - speed faster
	S - speed slower
	SPACE - shoot ball
	MOUSE_LEFT - change angle to position of mouse pointer

The game is a simple shooting game. You have to hit targets for getting score.
There are two levels for hitting target. First hit will oscillate the target and 
second hit will disappear the target. Targets are generated randomly after every 5 seconds.
You have maximum 20 balls to shoot. There are also 2 moving obstacles in the way.
